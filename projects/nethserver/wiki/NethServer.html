NethServer 6¶

NethServer is an operating system for the Linux enthusiast, designed for small offices and medium enterprises. It's simple, secure and flexible. www.nethserver.org

  • NethServer 6 reached EOL on November 30, 2020. It is recommended to upgrade to NethServer 7
