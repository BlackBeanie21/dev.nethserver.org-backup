Enhancement #1822

Mail-server: empty group delivery

Added by Davide Principi over 8 years ago. Updated about 8 years ago.

Status:CLOSEDStart date:04/12/2013
Priority:NormalDue date:04/12/2013
Assignee:-% Done:

100%

Category:nethserver-mail-server
Target version:v6.4-beta1
Resolution: NEEDINFO:

Description

Mail delivered to a group with no members must be saved somewhere. For MailDeliveryType=shared the mailbox is created when necessary and the message is delivered, but for MailDeliveryType=copy a fallback mechanism must be implemented.

For empty groups with MailDeliveryType=copy, deliver messages to postmaster account (admin).
