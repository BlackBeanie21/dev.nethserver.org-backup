From Fedora BugZappers/BugStatusWorkFlow

The resolution CURRENTRELEASE is to be used in the case where a bug is reported before a release is made, and subsequently discovered to be fixed in the final release. For instance, a bug is reported against Fedora 20 while it is still in the pre-release stages, and remains open when the release is made; however, when the final Fedora 20 is made, the reporter re-tests and discovers the bug was actually fixed. In this case, the CURRENTRELEASE resolution is used.
