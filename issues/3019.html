Pseudonyms no longer migrated

Added by Davide Principi over 6 years ago. Updated over 6 years ago.

Status:CLOSEDStart date:
Priority:NormalDue date:
Assignee:-% Done:

100%

Category:nethserver-mail-server
Target version:v6.5
Security class: Resolution:
Affected version:v6.5 NEEDINFO:No

Description

Starting from nethserver-mail-server-1.8.2, released on 2014-11-03 the pseudonym records are no longer migrated correctly.

Only user and group names are re-created as pseudonyms. Real pseudonyms records, such as name_surname of name.surname forms are ignored.

This bug arises from bugfix #2888.
